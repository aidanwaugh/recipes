Ingredients

  • eggs
  • sugar
  • butter, 1/2 lb
  • anise seeds
  • flour
  • baking powder
  • vanilla extract
  • almonds

Cooking Instructions

  1. Cream butter, sugar and eggs
  2. Add dry ingredients and seeds
  3. Pat the dough into long strips about 2 inches wide, one inch thick and as long as the sheet on which you will bake them
  4. Bake in a moderate oven for about 8 minutes, turn them over (without breaking if possible), and bake on the other side until lightly toasted looking.
  5. Remove to a cutting board and cut into diagonal slices about 1/2 inch wide
  6. Lay the slices on a cookie sheet cut-side up and bake until they are nicely browned (like toast) then turn with a "snatcher" and do the other side.

Notes

  • Biscotti as made by Vanda Creazzi. We are very fond of these with whole almonds which do not need to be blanched, and taste lovely when sliced through and toasted in the second cooking.
  • "biscotti" means twice cooked.
