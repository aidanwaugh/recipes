Ingredients

  • all-purpose flour
  • salt
  • double-acting baking powder
  • chilled butter, shortening, or combo, +/- 2tbsp
  • milk, +/- 1/4cup

Cooking Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 450°F
  2. Mix flour with salt and baking powder together then add butter
  3. Cut solid shorpp tening into dry ingredients with a pastry blender until the mixture is of the consistency of coarse corn meal.
  4. Make a well in the center of these ingredients.
  5. All at one add the milk
  6. Stir until the dough is fairly free from the sides of the bowl. The time for stirring should e scant 1/2 minute.
  7. Turn the dough onto a lightly floured board.
  8. Kneed gently and quickly for a scant 1/2 minute, making about 8 to 10 folds. Roll with a lightly floured rolling pin, until the dough has the desired thickness.
  9. Cut with a biscuit cutter dipped in a very little flour. Cut straight down with the cutter and be careful not to twist it.
  10. Brush the tops of the biscuits with milk or melted butter
  11. Place on ungreased baking sheet
  12. Bake until done 12- 15 minutes

Notes

  • Don't over work the dough or it won't be fluffy
  • Best served with honey on top
  • Also good with chicken pot pie
